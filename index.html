Coming from different musical backgrounds like hardcore, opera, classic rock or punkrock, our band has been brought together by the love of live music. Thus, our main intention is to perform as much as possible.
We are all well-trained and seasoned musicians having been in the music business for decades. We are not afraid of spotlights, different audiences and various spaces to stand on the stage and kick everybody's ass.
First we formed some popular pieces to our, and hopefully your, taste but we are planning to come up with the fruits of our personal creativity soon...
